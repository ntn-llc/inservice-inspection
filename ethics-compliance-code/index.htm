Ethics Compliance Code

Integrity

All IN-SERVICE INSPECTION COMPANY Employees are committed to perform their duties professional, independent and impartial manner in all its activities, honestly with no influence to allow any deviation from its approved methods and procedures. Where approved test methods make provision for tolerance in results, such tolerances shall not be abused to alter the actual test findings.

improperly changed, all reports and certificates shall correctly present the actual findings, professional opinions or results obtained.

Conflicts of Interest

IN-SERVICE INSPECTION COMPANY committed to ensure that conflicts of interest shall be avoided with any related entity in which there is any financial or commercial interest and to which services are provided. Conflicts of interest shall be avoided between the companies and/or divisions engaged in different activities but which may be providing services to either the same client or each other. Also IN-SERVICE INSPECTION COMPANY employees shall avoid conflicts of interest with the activities of the company.

Confidentiality

IN-SERVICE INSPECTION COMPANY Resources ensure that all information received in the course of the provision of its services shall be treated as business confidential to the extent that such information is not already published, generally available to third parties or otherwise in the public domain.

ln addition, all Information received should be protected as well, all information should be stored in a place that provide protection to this confidentiality.

Anti-Bribery

IN-SERVICE INSPECTION COMPANY Resources Prohibit any offer or acceptance of a bribe in any form, including kickbacks on any portion of a contract payment. Also the use of any routes or channels for provision of improper benefits to, or receipt of improper benefits from, customers, agents, contractors, suppliers, or employees of any such party, or government officials are prohibited.

Fair Business Conduct

IN-SERVICE INSPECTION COMPANY Resources shall only present itself and conduct marketing, including any comparisons with or references to competitors or their services, in a matter that is truthful and not deceptive or misleading or likely to mislead.

All Employees shall conduct business with the highest standards of business ethics and integrity and shall not do anything which would bring its reputation, or the reputation of IFIA / TIC Council or the TIC industry, into disrepute.

Health And Safety

IN-SERVICE INSPECTION COMPANY establish and implement safety training for both operations and health & Safety, all IN-SERVICE INSPECTION COMPANY employees is trained to protect the health and safety of employees, customers, and third parties and shall monitor incidents with the view of minimizing risks in the course of business operations.

Fair Labour

IN-SERVICE INSPECTION COMPANY is aware of its social responsibility for its employees and the people, communities and environments in which it works and shall respect human rights.
